# 🌙 NekoSleep

A minimalist productivity & health app that helps you actually go to sleep.

SleepLock asks you when you want to go to bed, and at the chosen time it automatically locks your computer for the next 8 hours — helping you avoid late-night scrolling, gaming, or endless “just 5 more minutes”.
___
✨ Features
* ⏰ Set your desired bedtime
* 🔒 Automatically locks your computer at that time
* 💤 Keeps the device locked for 8 hours
* 🧠 Helps build healthier sleep habits
* ⚡ Lightweight and distraction-free
* 🖥️ Works silently in the background
___
# ⚙️ How It Works
1. You choose your bedtime
2. NekoSleep waits in the background
3. At the selected time:
- your computer gets locked
- access is blocked for 8 hours
4. You wake up less sleep-deprived 👍
